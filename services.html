Services & Pricing

FacultyVA helps professors stay organized, respond faster, and keep courses running smoothly—without burning out. Choose a weekly support plan or start with a small bundle and scale up.

Core Services

Email & Student Communications

  • Inbox triage + priority sorting
  • Draft replies to student questions
  • Follow-up tracking (who needs what, by when)

LMS & Course Setup

  • Module organization and housekeeping
  • Due dates, timetables, calendar alignment
  • Announcements + weekly “what to do” guidance

Discussion Support

  • Draft instructor replies and prompts
  • Moderation support and escalation notes
  • Consistency with course tone and rubric

Feedback & Tutoring Support

  • Rubric-aligned feedback drafts
  • Editing support for clarity + structure
  • Optional tutoring templates and study guides

FAQ

Do you write posts as the professor?
I can draft responses for you to review and post, aligned with your tone and the rubric.

How do we work together?
We use a shared task list (Google Doc/Notion) and a weekly workflow. You delegate; I execute and report.

Can you work with Canvas/Blackboard/Moodle?
Yes. We’ll follow your institution’s access rules and keep everything secure.

How do I start?
Email me and I’ll send a short intake form + onboarding checklist.

Ready to lighten your workload?

Send a message and tell me what you teach, your LMS, and where you need support first.
